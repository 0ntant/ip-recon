Неверный формат адреса

Пожалуйста, используйте один из следующих форматов:

  • IPv4: например,
    {#if request} {request.scheme}://{request.host}/report?address=1.1.1.1
    {request.scheme}://{request.host}/report?address=1[.]1[.]1[.]1
    {request.scheme}://{request.host}/report?address=1.1[.]1.1 {#else} http://localhost:8080/report?address=1.1.1.1
    http://localhost:8080/report?address=1[.]1[.]1[.]1
    http://localhost:8080/report?address=1.1[.]1.1 {/if}
  • Домен: например,
    {#if request} {request.scheme}://{request.host}/report?address=google.com
    {request.scheme}://{request.host}/report?address=google[.]com
    {request.scheme}://{request.host}/report?address=https://www.google.com/
    {request.scheme}://{request.host}/report?address=https://www.google[.]com/ {#else} http://localhost:8080/report?address=google.com
    http://localhost:8080/report?address=google[.]com
    http://localhost:8080/report?address=https://www.google.com/
    http://localhost:8080/report?address=https://www.google[.]com/ {/if}
